I am Goose Rayos, a website designer. My designs are professional and help businesses and customers. A website is usually the first introduction between a customer and business, and a customer makes a decision very quickly whether to continue on with the relationship. My work ethic stays the same, no matter how big or small the task is.

Creativity is something I have a passion for. I love to create things that are appealing to the eye. I also like to solve problems through the help of design. I wish to continue designing websites for many years to come, and hope to meet many great people.

I'm originally for New Mexico, and moved to the great state of Texas for work. My wife and I have been here since early 2016, and looks like we'll be here for a long time. We have 2 dogs and enjoy taking walks, hiking, sports, traveling and more.
